Privacy Statement for lœrn App

Last Updated: February 5, 2026

Introduction

This privacy statement explains how lœrn ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information. lœrn is designed with privacy as a core principle. We believe your learning data should always remain under your control.

Except where you explicitly choose to export or share content, lœrn does not transmit your data outside your device.

Core Privacy Principles

Privacy-First Design

lœrn is built from the ground up to protect your privacy:

  • Offline-first by design: Core learning features work without an internet connection
  • No user accounts: No registration, no login, no passwords
  • No tracking: No behavioral analytics, no profiling
  • Local-only data storage: Learning data stays on your device unless you explicitly export or share it
  • User control: Data leaves your device only through actions you intentionally initiate

Data We Do Not Collect

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal information (name, email address, phone number, address)
  • User accounts or authentication credentials
  • Learning content or study materials you create or import
  • Learning progress, scores, or statistics
  • Usage analytics or telemetry data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data
  • Location data
  • Contact lists or social connections

For free users, advertising partners may receive limited technical data as required to display ads (see Advertising section below).

Data Storage

Local Storage Only

All learning-related data is stored exclusively on your device:

  • Learning decks and flashcards
  • Study progress and statistics
  • Review history and spaced repetition data
  • User preferences and settings
  • Local profile information (profile name, icon, language preference)

This data is stored in a local SQLite database within the app's private storage and is protected by your device's built-in security mechanisms (iOS Data Protection, Android Keystore).

No Cloud Synchronization

lœrn does not provide cloud sync, cloud backup, or server-side storage. We do not operate servers that receive or store your learning data.

Internet Connectivity

Offline Learning Core

The core learning functionality of lœrn is fully offline and does not require network access.

Advertising (Free Version Only)

If you use the free version of lœrn, the app displays advertisements. Only the advertising module accesses the internet in order to:

  • Load advertisement content
  • Report ad impressions and interactions
  • Comply with advertising network requirements

The learning core itself does not transmit learning data, content, or progress.

Advertising Partners

The free version uses Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain information according to its own privacy policy, which may include:

  • Device advertising identifier
  • IP address
  • Device type and operating system
  • Ad interaction data

For details on Google's data practices, see: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Premium Version

If you purchase the premium version (one-time purchase):

  • All advertisements are removed
  • The advertising module is not initialized
  • The app does not require internet access for normal use
  • No network communication is performed by the app

In-App Purchases

Purchase Processing

Premium purchases are processed entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play Store.

  • We do not collect or store payment information
  • We do not receive credit card or billing details
  • We only receive confirmation that a purchase was completed

Premium status is stored locally on your device. The purchase is tied to your App Store or Play Store account, not to any lœrn account.

Import, Export, and Sharing

Local File-Based Import and Export

lœrn allows you to import and export learning decks using JSON or ZIP files:

  • Imported files are processed locally on your device
  • Exported files contain only learning content (questions, answers, metadata)
  • Exported files do not include personal identifiers by default

User-Initiated Sharing

You may choose to share exported files using your device's standard sharing features.

Important clarifications:

  • Data is shared only when you explicitly initiate sharing
  • lœrn does not automatically transmit data
  • lœrn does not know who you share data with
  • Once shared, data is handled by the receiving app, service, or recipient according to their own privacy practices

You are solely responsible for how and where exported files are shared.

Copyright and Content Responsibility

lœrn is a tool, not a content provider. You are responsible for:

  • Ensuring you have the right to use imported content
  • Complying with copyright laws when sharing exported decks
  • The legality of any materials stored or shared using the app

Profiles

Multi-Profile Support

lœrn supports multiple local user profiles on a single device:

  • Each profile has isolated learning data
  • Profiles are identified only by a name and icon you choose
  • Profile data is stored locally and never transmitted by the app
  • Deleting a profile permanently removes its data from the device

Premium Status

Premium purchase status applies to all profiles on the same device and store account.

Notifications (Optional)

Learning Reminders

You may optionally enable learning reminders:

  • Reminders are scheduled locally on your device
  • No external servers are used for reminder delivery
  • You can disable reminders at any time
  • Notification permissions can be revoked in device settings

Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

Storage (Android)

  • Purpose: Importing and exporting deck files
  • Scope: Only files you explicitly select

Internet (Free Version Only)

  • Purpose: Displaying advertisements
  • Not used: Premium version

Notifications (Optional)

  • Purpose: Learning reminders
  • Control: You choose frequency and timing

Third-Party Services

Advertising (Free Version Only)

The free version uses Google AdMob.

We do not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Crash reporting services
  • Cloud storage providers
  • Social media integrations
  • Third-party tracking SDKs

Children's Privacy

lœrn is intended for university students and older learners. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction).

Because lœrn does not collect personal data, it is compliant with children's privacy regulations such as COPPA.

Data Security

Local Device Security

Your data is protected by:

  • Device-level encryption
  • App sandboxing
  • Your device's authentication mechanisms

No Server Risk

Because lœrn does not store data on servers, there is no risk of:

  • Server breaches
  • Cloud data leaks
  • Unauthorized third-party access

Your Responsibility

You are responsible for:

  • Securing your device
  • Managing device backups (iCloud / Google backup)
  • Safely storing exported files

Your Rights and Control

You have full control over your data:

  • All learning data is stored locally
  • You can export data at any time
  • You can delete decks or profiles
  • Uninstalling the app removes all local data

Data Retention

On-Device Only

Data remains on your device until you:

  • Delete content or profiles
  • Clear app data
  • Uninstall the app

No Server Retention

We do not retain data on servers because we do not operate any.

Device Backups

If enabled, your operating system may back up app data according to its own policies. This is controlled by Apple or Google, not by lœrn.

Legal Basis (GDPR)

For users in the EEA:

  • We do not process personal data as a controller
  • Advertising (free version) is based on legitimate interest
  • You can avoid advertising by purchasing the premium version

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

  • We do not sell personal information
  • We do not share personal information for marketing
  • Most CCPA obligations do not apply due to lack of data collection

International Data Transfers

We do not transfer learning data internationally because:

  • Data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not operate servers
  • Data leaves your device only through user-initiated sharing

Changes to This Statement

We may update this statement to reflect app or legal changes.

We will notify users by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Posting the updated statement
  • Displaying an in-app notice for material changes

Commitment to Privacy

lœrn is built on the belief that learning should be private by default. Your data stays under your control, and you decide if and when it is shared.

  • Free version shows ads (AdMob may collect ad-related data)
