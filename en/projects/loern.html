lœrn

A learning app built around a simple idea: you bring the content, we provide thoughtful methods.

Get it on Google Play Privacy statement

Offline-first, no accounts, and data stays on-device unless you export or share.

What it is

Instead of prescribing what to learn, lœrn focuses on how to learn. Create your own decks, train with different learning methods, and track your progress in a way that stays calm and transparent.

Features

▦

Create & organize study decks

Build decks for anything you’re learning and keep them tidy.

⟲

Train with different learning modes

Use multiple methods to reinforce understanding and recall.

▣

Stats & progress

See what’s improving and where you want to focus next.

⇅

Import/Export for backup & sharing

Keep backups locally and share decks when you choose.

✎

Bring your own content

Your learning material, your structure, your pace.

✦

AI-assisted content creation

Use AI where it helps with clarity and structure—always transparent.
