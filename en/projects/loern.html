lœrn

Concept

lœrn is a learning application built around a simple idea: you bring the content, we provide the methods.

Instead of prescribing what to learn, lœrn focuses on how to learn. It offers structured approaches to working with material you choose.

AI as assistance

AI is used where it can help clarify or organize, not where it replaces understanding. The application assists with structure and suggestions while keeping you in control of the learning process.

There are no black boxes. When AI is involved, its role is clear and its output is transparent.

Philosophy

Learning requires focus and agency. lœrn is designed to support both.

No distracting features. No gamification for its own sake. No pressure to engage daily. The application exists to serve your learning goals, not to create dependency.

Availability

lœrn is currently in development and will be released on the Play Store and App Store. No subscription required.
